HealthSailBuddy Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

HealthSailBuddy is a WhatsApp-based health information assistant. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains in simple language what data we collect, how we use it, and how we protect it.

1. What HealthSailBuddy Does

HealthSailBuddy provides general health-related information and service guidance through WhatsApp. It does not provide medical diagnosis, treatment, or medical advice.

Any responses generated by AI are for informational and educational purposes only. They are not a substitute for professional medical consultation. Always contact a qualified healthcare professional for medical concerns.

2. Information We Collect

When you use HealthSailBuddy on WhatsApp, we may receive:

  • Your phone number (provided by WhatsApp)
  • Messages you send to the assistant
  • Files or images you choose to upload (such as reports)
  • Basic interaction data (timestamps, message status)

You should only share information you are comfortable sharing through a messaging platform.

3. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Respond to your questions
  • Provide health-related information
  • Improve system performance and quality
  • Maintain system safety and reliability

We do not sell your data. We do not use your data for advertising. We do not share your personal data with marketers.

4. WhatsApp Cloud API

HealthSailBuddy operates through the WhatsApp Cloud API. Messages you send are processed through WhatsApp’s systems and then delivered to our systems for response generation.

Your use of WhatsApp is also governed by WhatsApp’s own privacy policy.

5. Temporary File Storage

If you upload medical files or images:

  • They are stored temporarily for processing
  • They are automatically deleted after processing or within a limited retention period
  • They are not used for advertising or sold

We encourage users not to upload unnecessary sensitive documents.

6. Data Retention

We keep data only as long as needed to operate the service, improve quality, and meet legal or safety requirements. Data may be deleted periodically or upon request where feasible.

7. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect data. This includes access controls and secure infrastructure.

However, no system can be guaranteed 100% secure. By using the service, you understand this risk.

8. Children’s Privacy

HealthSailBuddy is not designed for children under 13. Parents or guardians should supervise any use by minors.

9. Your Choices

You can:

  • Stop using the service at any time
  • Avoid sharing sensitive information
  • Request deletion of prior messages where feasible

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The latest version will always be posted on this page.

11. Contact

For privacy questions, you may contact: HealthSailBuddy Support
Email: support@healthsailbuddy.com

By using HealthSailBuddy, you agree to this Privacy Policy.
